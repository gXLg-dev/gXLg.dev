Privacy Policy
1. General Information
I take the protection of your personal data seriously. This privacy policy informs you about the collection, use, and processing of personal data when using my website.
2. Use of Cloudflare Turnstile
To protect my website from bots and abuse, I use Cloudflare Turnstile, a service provided by Cloudflare Inc.. Cloudflare may process your IP address and other technical data. More information can be found in Cloudflare's privacy policy.
3. Use of Cloudflare Tunnel
To enhance security and ensure reliable access, this website is routed through Cloudflare Tunnel, a service provided by Cloudflare Inc.. This means that all traffic between visitors and my server passes through Cloudflare's network. Cloudflare may process technical data such as IP addresses and request metadata. For more details, please refer to Cloudflare's privacy policy.
4. Use of Stripe Payment Services
I offer payment processing via Stripe (Stripe Inc.). However, I only provide a link to Stripe's payment portal, and no payment data is processed directly on my website. For details on Stripe's data processing, please visit Stripe's privacy policy.
5. Use of Google Fonts
My website uses Google Fonts to ensure consistent and visually appealing typography. The fonts are loaded via @import from Google's servers. When you access my website, your browser may send a request to Google, which could result in the processing of your IP address and other technical data. For more information, please refer to Google's privacy policy.
6. Use of Third-Party Icons
My website uses icons from the following providers:
  • Material Symbols by Google, which may be loaded from Google's servers. More details: Google's privacy policy.
  • DevIcons and Remix Icons, which are hosted on jsDelivr (a public CDN). When loading these icons, your browser may establish a connection to jsDelivr's servers. More details: jsDelivr's privacy policy.
7. Cookies Used
I use three technical cookies on my website:
  • token: Used for authentication. This cookie stores a hashed version of the user's IP address. No data is transferred to third parties, and it is processed only on my server. The IP hash is not stored on the server.
  • state: Used to manage error and success messages.
  • lang: Stores the user's language preference.
These cookies are essential for the technical functionality of the website and do not require user consent under the GDPR.
8. Storage and Processing of Personal Data
I store personal data only in the following context:
  • Email Logs: I keep records of all sent emails for technical monitoring and verification of my mail service's functionality. These logs contain personal data such as email addresses and are deleted monthly. The data is not shared with third parties.
9. Data Subject Rights
Under the GDPR, you have the right to:
  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify incorrect data
  • Request data deletion
  • Restrict processing
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability (if applicable)
To exercise these rights, please contact me at gxlg@gmx.de.
10. Contact
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact me at gxlg@gmx.de.
